About Us

Who We Are

We are 3rd-year undergraduate students from IIT Tirupati, united by a shared passion for making a difference. Through our innovative project, we aim to contribute to solving sustainable development goals by equipping people with disaster preparedness and management skills.

Our Vision

To build a safer, more resilient world by empowering individuals with practical knowledge and strategies for disaster response through interactive simulations.

What We Offer

Disaster simulation platform allows users to experience real-life disaster scenarios, make informed decisions, and learn essential survival skills. Scenarios like earthquakes, floods, and tornado help users gain the tools to act effectively during crises.

Our Mission

By combining cutting-edge technology and creative solutions, our project strives to raise awareness and build a community of disaster-smart individuals, fostering resilience and sustainability.

Join Us

We believe in the power of education and technology to drive meaningful change. Join us in this journey to prepare, learn, and create a positive impact for a sustainable future.
